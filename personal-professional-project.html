Personal Professional Project

Connecting with professionals is one of the best ways to prepare effectively for entering the job market after my studies. To do this, I use platforms such as LinkedIn and MyJobGlasses to reach out to engineers and managers working in the transportation sector.

Thanks to MyJobGlasses, I had the opportunity to exchange with professionals from major companies in this field: my internship at TE Connectivity (sensors and connectivity solutions for automotive and heavy vehicles) and discussions with two professionals from Thales in the aeronautics and aerospace domain. These conversations helped me better understand the daily reality of engineers and the expectations of large industrial groups.
