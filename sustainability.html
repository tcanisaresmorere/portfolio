Sustainability & Civic Engagement

At ENSEEIHT, students are encouraged to take part in activities that raise awareness about global challenges and social responsibility. During my first year, I had the opportunity to participate in two major initiatives.

🌍 Climate Fresk

The Climate Fresk is a collaborative workshop designed to raise awareness about climate change by connecting scientific data with real-world impacts. In small groups, we built a visual map of the causes and consequences of global warming.

This activity allowed me to deepen my understanding of environmental issues. It also highlighted the role that engineers must play in building sustainable solutions for the future.

♿ Disability Awareness Day

The Disability Awareness Day was particularly meaningful to me on a personal level. Having a younger brother with autism, I have always been deeply aware of the importance of inclusion and understanding towards people with disabilities.

I strongly believe that such initiatives are a real opportunity for students, as they encourage us to develop empathy and openness. I am sincerely grateful that ENSEEIHT supports these values and actively promotes awareness of the challenges faced by people with disabilities.
