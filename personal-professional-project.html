Personal Professional Project (PPP)

Over time, I've realized that connecting with mentors and engaging with industry professionals is one of the best ways to stay updated on the job market and its expectations. To achieve this, I rely on a range of tools and strategies, particularly LinkedIn and MyJobGlasses, which allow me to reach out to engineers and managers from different sectors.

Career Interests

I am open to diverse professional sectors, but two domains particularly stand out for me: transportation (aeronautics and aerospace included) and finance. What connects these areas is my strong interest in applied mathematics, as both offer opportunities to work with algorithms, data, and optimization.

Aeronautics and aerospace fascinate me because of their technological challenges and their contribution to global mobility. This is why I first sought to interview professionals working in this sector. At the same time, the finance industry also attracts me, as it combines mathematical rigor and advanced computing, particularly in algorithmic trading and risk analysis.

Connecting with Mentors

Thanks to MyJobGlasses, I was able to connect with professionals from leading companies. I had the chance to interview Yan Frick (Head of Digital and IS, Thales Flight Line Avionics BU) and Yohann P. (Engineering Delivery Manager, Thales). They provided valuable insights into career opportunities, the importance of adaptability, and the skills sought by major companies in the aeronautics sector.

Transport Industry & Internship Experience

The transportation sector fascinates me, not only because of its technological innovations but also because of its strong societal impact. This is one of the reasons I chose to complete my internship at TE Connectivity, an international leader in connectivity and sensor solutions.

TE Connectivity manufacturing facility in Toulouse

At the Toulouse site, TE Connectivity manufactures a wide range of sensors, including those for humidity, temperature, viscosity, fluid characteristics (such as oil), and speed measurement. These applications are essential to ensure the safety, efficiency, and performance of vehicles and industrial systems. This internship gave me valuable insights into the transport industry and reinforced my motivation to contribute to this exciting field.
